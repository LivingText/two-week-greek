Lesson objectives

τέλοι τοῦ μαθήματος

  • ; = Greek question mark
  • ναί = yes, οὔ = no
  • ἤ = or
  • τίς; = who?
  • πότε; = when?
  • Basic understanding of John 1:1-2 (relax, you won't understand it at first, our job is to teach this).
