τί ἐστιν γραφὴ ζῶσα;

What is γραφὴ ζῶσα?

  • An immersive course in Hellenistic Greek
  • Listening, Speaking, Reading, Writing

What can I expect?

  • Hearing more Greek than you can understand in each lesson
  • Always knowing what is expected of you and what to do next
  • Becoming increasingly competent in understanding Greek
  • Hearing less and less English as the course progresses

Why should I study Greek this way?

  • Deeper Understanding
  • Better Retention
